About Us

We study how climate and weather affect human health, with a focus on temperature extremes, air pollution, and other environmental stressors. Our research lies at the intersection of epidemiology, human biometeorology, and data science, with particular attention to urban populations and climate change.

Research Focus

  • Climate change, temperature extremes, and mortality
  • Predictive models for future health risks
  • Adaptation strategies and public health policy
