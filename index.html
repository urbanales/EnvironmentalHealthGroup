About Us

We study how climate and weather affect human health, focusing on temperature extremes, air pollution, and other environmental stressors at the intersection of epidemiology, biometeorology, and data science.

Research Focus

  • Climate change, temperature extremes, and mortality
  • Predictive models for future health risks
  • Adaptation strategies and public health policy
